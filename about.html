Mission Statement

Build to Last

We believe growth is not something you chase. It's something you cultivate.It begins with founders who are driven by purpose. Who build not simply to sell, but to solve meaningful problems, to improve lives, and to create something that endures.

These brands don't grow through chance or noise. They grow through clarity, conviction, and deliberate action.

Growth, like anything enduring, requires the right conditions. The right foundation. The right timing. The right care.We exist to help create those conditions.

Not through isolated tactics or temporary wins, but through thoughtful strategy rooted in purpose, guided by experience, and designed to scale. Because growth without foundation is fragile. And performance without brand is forgettable.

We believe founders deserve partners who think like owners. Who ask the hard questions. Who take responsibility for outcomes, not just deliverables.

Because sustainable growth compounds. And brands built with intention are the ones that endure.

This is why Cultiva exists. To help purpose-driven brands grow with intention, build foundations designed to scale, and turn vision into momentum, and momentum into lasting impact.

Meet the Founder

Raul bridges the gap between brand and performance marketing

Cultiva Studio was founded by Raul Pero — a brand strategist, Fractional CMO, and former CPG executive who has spent more than two decades building consumer brands across Food, Beverage, Wellness, and Home & Personal Care.

He's held senior marketing roles at Planterra Foods and J.R. Watkins, worked acrossorganizations ranging from Fortune 500 companies to family-owned businesses to venture-backed startups, and co-founded Terraseed, a DTC-first sustainable supplement brand, where he built the growth engine from scratch, navigated the hard calls on positioning and spend, and learned firsthand what it takes to scale something you've built with your own hands.

That co-founder experience shapes everything about how Cultiva works. It means we don't advise from a distance. We understand the pressure of limited resources, the uncertainty of which lever to pull, and the cost of making the wrong move at the wrong time, because we've felt it too. We bring that lived experience into every engagement, alongside the rigor and strategic discipline of someone who's also operated at the highest levels of the industry.

He doesn't consult from a distance. He partners like an owner, asking the hard questions, taking responsibility for outcomes, and staying focused on what actually moves the business forward.

Raul currently serves on the Board of Naturally Colorado and is actively engaged in the broader natural products community.
